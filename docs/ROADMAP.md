# InvestLion – Project Roadmap

This roadmap outlines the phased development plan for the InvestLion ecosystem.
Timelines are indicative and may adjust based on development progress and ecosystem readiness.

---

## Phase 1 – Foundation (Current)

**Objective:** Establish transparency, trust, and core structure.

Deliverables:
- Verified ILION smart contract on BNB Smart Chain
- Public GitHub repository with open documentation
- Vision, mission, and core values defined
- Tokenomics and supply policy documented
- Community communication channels established
- Utility planning and internal testing

Status: In progress

---

## Phase 2 – Utility Expansion

**Objective:** Introduce real use cases for ILION within the ecosystem.

Planned deliverables:
- Platform access features powered by ILION
- Internal rewards and incentive mechanisms
- Referral participation framework
- Controlled token distribution aligned with usage
- Continuous documentation updates

Status: Planned

---

## Phase 3 – Ecosystem Growth

**Objective:** Scale usage and strengthen the ecosystem.

Planned deliverables:
- Additional platform features
- Community growth initiatives
- Strategic partnerships and integrations
- Improved infrastructure for higher activity
- Evaluation of liquidity strategy aligned with real demand

Status: Planned

---

## Phase 4 – Long-Term Vision

**Objective:** Prepare for larger ecosystem evolution.

Potential initiatives:
- Multi-chain integrations
- Appchain or layer-2 exploration (branding-focused)
- Advanced governance or participation models
- Long-term sustainability mechanisms

Status: Vision stage

---

## Roadmap Principles

- Utility before price
- Transparency before promotion
- Sustainable growth over short-term gains
- Community trust as the highest priority

---

## Disclaimer

This roadmap is subject to change.
InvestLion does not promise returns, profits, or guaranteed outcomes.
All development decisions prioritize ecosystem health and long-term value.
